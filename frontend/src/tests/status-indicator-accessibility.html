Task 9.3: Verify status indicators have non-color cues

Requirements 10.4 & 10.5

Test how status indicators work for color-blind users
Test Purpose: This page demonstrates that all status indicators include non-color cues (icons, text labels, symbols) in addition to color. Click "Toggle Grayscale Mode" to simulate color blindness and verify that all information remains accessible.

1. Toast Notifications

✅ With Color & Icons
